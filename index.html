Our Legacy

A Family-Run Business Since 1950

Welcome to Vastu Ply Lam, your most trusted destination for premium hardware, furniture, plywood, and more. What started in 1950 as a humble local shop has blossomed into a comprehensive hub for homeowners, builders, interior designers, and contractors.

Through three generations of family leadership, our core values have remained unshaken: uncompromised material quality, honest pricing, and deep roots in our community.

  • Over 7 decades of market expertise
  • Sourced from world-class manufacturers
  • Dedicated assistance for large projects
